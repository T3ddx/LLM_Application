Mathematics/Business Dual (Applied Mathematics, Mathematics, or Statistics)
The program is a collaboration between the Mathematics Department and the Whitman School of Management.  Students have the opportunity to be dually enrolled in The College of Arts and Sciences and Whitman while selecting any major in Whitman paired with either Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, or Statistics from within The College of Arts and Sciences. Students complete the requirements for the B.S. degree from the College of Arts and Sciences, including at least 90 credits in Arts and Sciences coursework. Students also satisfy requirements for a major in the Martin J. Whitman School of Management.
